# Project Brief: PreClinic

## Core Requirements & Goals
PreClinic is an autonomous clinic assistant platform designed to bridge the data communication gap between patients and doctors. 
- **Patient Side (Mobile App):** Allows patients to enter symptoms in natural language. An AI system processes this to recommend a department (e.g., Cardiology, Neurology) and maps details to structured medical standards.
- **Doctor Side (Web Dashboard):** Translates patient symptoms into structured, international standard SOAP (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan) formatted anamnesis logs.
- **AI Medical Memory:** Uses vector/similarity matching to connect historical patient records with current symptom entries to alert the doctor of anomalies or critical indicators.
- **Post-discharge Follow-up:** Continuously monitors patient recovery via scheduled automated query systems and notifies doctors if parameters deviate.

## Target Audience
- **Hospitals & Clinics:** Seeking efficient patient routing and higher appointment throughput.
- **Doctors:** Seeking automated SOAP entry reduction and clear historical anomaly detection.
- **Patients:** Struggling to identify the correct department, or missing symptoms during live consultation.
